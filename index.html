How to Make a Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:

  1. Peanut Butter
  2. Jelly (any flavor you like!)
  3. Bread (white, wheat, or your favorite kind)

Instructions:

  1. Spread peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  3. Press the two slices together, cut if desired, and enjoy!
